ARGENTINA

"Argentina is famous for being the birthplace of Tango"

The tango, the beautiful and passionate dance that originated from Argentina, the thundering waterfalls of Iguazu Falls that put all other waterfalls to shame, and the hikes in Patagonia at the edge of the world will take your breath away at every turn.

PERU

"Peru is famous for Ayahuasca ceremonies and Shamans in Amazon."

A country with a diversity and wealth unlike any country in the world. With the archaeological patrimony of the Inca's empire, amazing food, and the most beautiful part of the Andes, Peru is its own league of South America.

AUSTRALIA

"Australia is globally famous for its natural wonders, wide-open spaces, beaches, deserts, "The Bush", and "The Outback"."

Australia is a huge country with many things to offer for travelers who have the time to really dive deep into all the things Australia has to offer from the wild and colorful landscapes.

NEW ZEALAND

"Manuka honey is produced by bees foraging on the flowers of the Manuka tree, a very special tree that only grows in New Zealand."

A country with stunning nature and diverse landscapes that was designed by the people who love nature, enjoy hiking and being among wildlife.

INDIA

"India is known for many, many different aspects such as food, culture, its massive population."

An extremely diverse country with unique and vastly differences in landscape, climate, culture, language, and ethnicity. India will overload your senses like no other country in the world.
